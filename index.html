Privacy Policy for Talking Tree

Last updated: February 2026

Talking Tree ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our young users. This app is designed for children and families.

1. Information Collection

We do NOT collect any personal information (like names, emails, or locations) from our users.

2. Use of Camera and Photos

The app requires access to your camera and gallery ONLY to identify plants. Images are sent to PlantNet API for identification and are NOT stored on our servers.

3. External Services

We use Firebase (Remote Config), PlantNet (Plant ID), and DeepSeek (Story Generation). These services do not receive any personal data from the app.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.
